Travel Tips for a Smooth Journey

Best Time to Visit

Spring and Fall are ideal times to visit Taiwan due to mild weather conditions, perfect for outdoor activities.

Spring Climate characteristics:

The climate is mild, coinciding with the rainy season, rainy and humid, and the temperature is about 20-25 ¬∞C.

Getting Around

MRT

Taiwan has efficient MRT systems in cities like Taipei and Kaohsiung.

High-Speed Rail

Connects major cities swiftly, offering a convenient travel option.

Train

Taiwan's train system is a reliable and efficient way to travel across the island.

Buses

It offers frequent services with clear route information, while intercity buses connect various regions across the island.

Taxi

Taxis in Taiwan are a convenient and reliable mode of transportation, especially for short trips within cities.

Language

The official language in Taiwan is Mandarin Chinese, but English is commonly spoken in tourist areas.

Currency

The official currency is the New Taiwan Dollar (NTD). ATMs and currency exchange counters are widely available.

Safety

  • General Safety Tips: Taiwan is generally safe for travelers. Exercise usual precautions.
  • Emergency Numbers: Police: 110, Ambulance and Fire: 119.
